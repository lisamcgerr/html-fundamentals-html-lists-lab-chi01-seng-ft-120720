• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Mozarella
    • Pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
    1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
    2. Place bread in the frying pan and fry
    3. Add cheese on top of bread
    4. Cover with second slice of bread
    5. Turn over & fry for 2 minutes
